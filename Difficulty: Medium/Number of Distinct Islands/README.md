<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/number-of-distinct-islands/1">Number of Distinct Islands</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Given a grid <strong>grid[][] </strong>of size <strong>n × m</strong>, consisting of characters <strong>'L'</strong> and <strong>'W'</strong>, where 'L' represents Land and 'W' represents Water, find the number of distinct islands in the grid. An island is a group of one or more land cells connected horizontally or vertically.</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Two islands are considered distinct if their shapes are different.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Two islands have the same shape if one can be translated to match the other exactly. Rotation and reflection are not allowed.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Examples :</span></strong></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="font-size: 18px;">Input: </strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">grid[][] = [['L', 'W', 'W'], ['W', 'W', 'L'], ['L', 'W', 'W']]</span><strong style="font-size: 18px;"><br></strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Output:</span> </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">1</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The grid contains three islands. All these islands have the same shape (a 1 × 1 block of land), so they are counted as a single distinct island.<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/929872/Web/Other/blobid1_1781788753.png" width="133" height="131"></span> </pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">grid[][] = [['L', 'L', 'W', 'L', 'L'], ['L', 'W', 'W', 'W', 'W'], ['W', 'W', 'L', 'W', 'L'], ['L', 'W', 'W', 'L', 'L']]</span><br><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Output:</span> </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">4</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong>There are five islands in the grid. Two islands have the same shape (a 1 × 1 block of land), while the other three have different shapes. Therefore, the number of distinct island shapes is 4.<br><strong><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/929872/Web/Other/blobid3_1781788840.png" width="217" height="173"></strong></span>
</pre>
